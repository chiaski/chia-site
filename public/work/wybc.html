About Work Résumé
Redesigning Yale's student radio, founded 1944.

I'm leading the design and engineering to overhaul WYBC, Yale's student radio. On Broadway Avenue in New Haven, Connecticut—hundreds of students come in and out to broadcast radio, write zines, throw shows (not in the same place), and create via radio for the Yale Community.
Being a web-only radio and with many COVID-19 adjustmments, I stepped forward and took initiative in bringing WYBC to the modern age. The redesign is currently in progress, but I'll let you know my thoughts and screens here along the way.

Roles Design and front-end engineering Tools Figma, Framer X, Vue, and a lot more
Long live radio!
